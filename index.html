Engine Manual

Parameter Breakdown

COSMIC MODE: Dreamy, cinematic sequence generation. Uses strict rhythmic algorithms (Fibonacci, Tesla 3-6-9) and applies ancient Just Intonation tuning via sequential pitchwheel micro-bending. No block chords.

Cosmic Flow: Controls the density and speed of the sequence. <40% = Nebula (Sparse/Dreamy), 40-70% = Orbit (Pulsing), >70% = Quantum (Rolling/Fast).

Glissando (Micro-Strum): Spawns rapid 3 to 6-note cascades. The engine enforces an absolute 25ms physical delay between notes to sound human. The backend limits this to maximum 1 strum per bar to prevent muddy overlapping.

Style: Defines the overarching genre, phrasing, and feeling of the MIDI.

Key & Mode: Sets the tonal center and musical scale the generation adheres to.

Tempo & Bars: Determines the speed (BPM) and the total length of the sequence.

Rhythmic Grid: Sets the internal beat subdivision (Straight, Triplets, Quintuplets, Polyrhythm).

Humanize / Swing: Shifts notes slightly off-grid to create a natural, human micro-groove.

Harmonic Density: Scales complexity from simple monophonic lines (Lvl 1) to lush, complex jazz chords (Lvl 5).
